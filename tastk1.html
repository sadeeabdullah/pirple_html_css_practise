My favourite food is noodles. Here is the processure to make it:

Ingredients needed:

  • Noodles
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Egg
  • Chilli
  • Onion
  • cooking oil

How to make it :

  1. At first boil the Noodles.
  2. Then remove the water from it.
  3. Put a frying pan on the stove and take oil on it.
  4. Add onion and Chilli on it and after these add the egg
  5. Then add the boiled noodles and cook it for a few minutes.
  6. At last the noodles is ready to serve.
